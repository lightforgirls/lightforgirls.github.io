Program description

This free beginner-level course provides a strong foundation in computer literacy and essential Windows and Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, and PowerPoint).
It is designed as an entry point for Afghan girls who wish to continue their learning journey toward more advanced computer and digital skill programs.
Upon successful completion, participants will receive an officially recognized certificate issued from the institution validating their skills and achievements.

Gender: Female
Level: Beginner
Number of opportunities: 30
Language of Instruction: Dari
Fund Type: Scholarship-based
Target Group: Afghan girls and women with little or no prior computer knowledge who are interested in developing essential digital literacy skills.

Selection Criteria

1. Gender: Applicants must be female.
2. Nationality: Priority will be given to Afghan girls living in Afghanistan.
3. Age group: Preferred age range is 15–25 years old.
4. Education level: At least basic literacy (reading and writing skills) is required.
5. Tools: Applicants should have access to a personal or shared computer/laptop and stable internet connection.
6. Motivation: Applicants must demonstrate strong interest and commitment to learning digital skills.
7. Availability: Must be able to attend five classes per week for the full duration of the course.
8. Financial background: Priority will be given to applicants from low-income families or those with limited access to formal education opportunities.

Mode of Delivery

Duration: 7 weeks
Frequency: 5 sessions per week (each session 60 minutes)
Format: Online (via Zoom/Google Meet)
Language of instruction: Dari
Teaching method: Combination of live classes, practical exercises, and weekly assignments
Assessment: Final practical test and continuous class participation

Benefits

Participants who attend classes regularly and pass the final assessment will receive an official and digital certificate issued from the organization.
Graduates will be eligible to join the next level of advanced computer and digital skills courses.
